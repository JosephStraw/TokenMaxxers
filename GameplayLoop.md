#These are the ideas for our Token Maxxer's Holiday Tycoon
- 2D perspective
*inspo from Chris Sawyer's Rollercoaster Tycoon*

#Carbon credit feature
- Scale of 0-10, 10 being 100% envrionmentally friendly, 0 being 1980's China
- this scale is affected by the types of visitor that are attracted to the holiday park

#Types of visitors
- There are 3 types of visitors that spend different amounts of money, and affect the envrionmental friendliness of the park
- The Hippie (very environmentally friendly) The average Joe (smaller affect on envrionmentally friendliness) and the fat cat (spends money, very bad envrionmentally)
- Its up to the player to build holiday buildings, gift shops and other entertainment venues to cater to all 3 whilst balancing their carbon footprint.

#gameplay loop
- player starts with starting cash, 1 basic building, carbon credit in the green
- player must attract holidayers by building envrionmentally friendly and unfriendly buildings to attract vistors and make money
- they must keep their envrionemnt meter within balance to avoid more taxes (taken at regular intervals from the players cash and makes buildings more expensive)
- 

#gameplay win/lose condition
- gameplay loop continues until player gets 100,000 money and maximum envrionmentally friendliness to build final earth statue
- gameplay loop stops when player cannot pay taxes
